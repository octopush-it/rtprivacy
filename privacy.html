Privacy Policy

Privacy at Retail Therapy

Effective date: 05/01/2026

Retail Therapy is a simulated shopping app. We've designed it to be one of the most privacy-respecting apps on the App Store: we don't ask for your personal information, we don't collect data about you, and we don't track you across apps or websites.

What we don't do

  • We do not ask you to create an account.
  • We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information.
  • We do not have a backend server that stores your information.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not display advertisements.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not sell or share data — there is no data to sell or share.

What we do

All your activity is stored on your device only. Your wishlist, cart, order history, persona, and Retail Therapy Card simulation state are saved to your phone's local storage. They never leave your device.

We serve product images from a content delivery network (Cloudflare R2). Like any website that loads images, your device's IP address is briefly visible to the CDN as part of the standard HTTPS request. We do not log, store, or analyze that IP. Cloudflare's standard CDN logging does not include personally identifying information beyond what's necessary to deliver the response, and we do not retain or query those logs ourselves.

We send local notifications about your simulated orders. These are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device — no server is contacted to send them. You can disable them at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → Retail Therapy.

The credit card simulation is not a financial product. The "Retail Therapy Card" feature uses obviously-fake card numbers (Stripe's well-known 4242 test BIN) and a "DEMO" watermark on the card visual. It cannot be linked to a real bank, exchanged for real currency, or used outside this app. No real money is involved at any point.

Data deletion

Because no data leaves your device, "deletion" is simple: delete the app. All local state is removed automatically. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.

Children

Retail Therapy is rated 4+ and contains no content unsuitable for children. We do not knowingly collect data from children, because we do not collect data from anyone.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email team.retail.therapy@pm.me.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the effective date above. The current version always lives at this URL.
